Key Notes

Types 7 & 8 (Electric / e-Power) — enter motor kW in the capacity field, not cc. Rates updated per GN 2421/42 (01.02.2025).

Type 8 (Range Extender / e-Power) — petrol engine drives only a generator on board; the electric motor always drives the wheels. e.g. Nissan Note e-Power, Nissan Serena e-Power, BMW i3 Rex, Mazda MX-30 R-EV. HS codes 8703.80.51–.64. Same rates as BEV.

Types 10, 11, 13, 14 — fixed per-unit XID regardless of engine size. Any cc value can be entered.

CID = 20% of CIF directly. CIF × 110% is used only as part of the VAT base (GN 2421/41) — not for CID. Type 8 (e-Power) rates updated per GN 2434/04.

All figures are approximate. Confirm with your clearing agent. Sources: GN 2418-43 (10.01.2025) + GN 2421/41-43 (31.01.2025).

Tax Formula
XID = Tax Rate per cc/kW × cc/kW (or fixed per-unit amount)
CID = CIF × 30% (GN 2478/03, eff. 01.04.2026)
SUR = CID × 50% (zero for Electric, e-Power, and Bus)
VAT Base = (CIF × 110%) + XID + CID + SUR (GN 2421/41)
VAT = VAT Base × 18%
Luxury Tax= MAX(0 , CIF LKR − Threshold) × Luxury Rate
VEL = Rs.15,000 (flat, all vehicles)
PAL = CIF × 110% × 7.5% (buses only)
SSL = CIF × 110% × 2.5% (buses only)
SSCL = (CIF × 110% + XID + CID + SUR) × 2.5% (eff. 01.05.2026 — Amendment Act No.10/2026)

TOTAL DUTY = XID + CID + SUR + VAT + Luxury Tax + VEL (+ PAL + SSL for buses)
TOTAL COST = CIF LKR + TOTAL DUTY
